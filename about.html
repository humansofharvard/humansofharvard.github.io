About

HUMANS OF HARVARD aims to capture the stories of the Harvard community and to spread gratitude within the community. At any given time, you can submit a letter of gratitude to our website, and before the first Thursday of each month, we will print and mail the notes of gratitudes. We will also be around campus and will post a few stories a week. We hope that Humans of Harvard can make your day a little brighter.

FAQ

Who can submit a story or gratitude letter?

Although we currently act as a platform for members of the Harvard community, anyone can!

What do we feature?

We feature both stories and letters of gratitude on our FB page and website.

  • STORIES: We hope that stories give people a chance to express anything they might want to share, whether it be advice for others, life lessons learned, or funny moments that just happened.
  • LETTERS OF GRATITUDE: We hope that the letters of gratitude will give people the opportunity to show gratitude to others. This brightens the day of not just the recipient of the letter, but often also the sender!

How do I participate?

To submit a story or gratitude letter, simply head to 'Submit' via the menu.

Can submissions be anonymous?

Yes!

How do we contact you?

Drop your name, email, and message down in the footer! We'll try to get back to you as soon as possible!
