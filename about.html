About

Humans of Harvard aims to catch a glimpse into the stories of the Harvard community and to pay forward tidbits of gratitude by letting people know they are appreciated. At any given time, you can submit a letter of gratitude to our website, and every few stories, we’ll meet with individuals to find out who they are thankful for.. By the first Thursday of each month, we will print and deliver all the notes of gratitude both from our conversations and our website as well as add your story to the chain of other thankful notes on our Facebook page. We hope that Humans of Harvard can make your day a little brighter.

FAQs

Who can submit a story or gratitude letter?

Although we currently act as a platform for members of the Harvard community, anyone can!

What do we feature?

We have both stories and gratitude letters on our FB page and website.

  • STORIES : We hope that stories give you the chance to express anything about your life that you feel comfortable sharing. These stories can be about anything - maybe a rough time you went through, some advice you wish to share with others, or perhaps just something that happened to you today.
  • GRATITUDE LETTERS : These letters are our aim to pass along some tidbit of appreciation to someone you might not always acknowledge. By letting someone else know that he or she matters to you, not only can you feel more grateful but also the other person feels loved.
Humans of Harvard strives to be a safe space that promotes happiness and well-being in the Harvard community.

How does this work?

If you want to submit a story or a gratitude letter, just click on the 'Submit' tab in the Menu bar. Fill out the relevant information for whichever submission, story or gratitude letter. If you fill out a gratitude letter, there is additional information for you to read and fill out.

Can submissions be anonymous?

Stories can definitely be anonymous if you want them to! Gratitude letters can be anonymous too but we hope that you will fill out your name so the person who you appreciate will know that you appreciate them!

How do we contact you?

Drop your name, email, and message down in the footer of any of the website pages and we'll try to get back to you as soon as possible!
